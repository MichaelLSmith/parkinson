Parkinson Canada Office

We Offer You...

  • continuing education and resources for health professionals at ParkinsonClinicalGuidelines.ca
  • a toll-free information and referral line at 1 (800) 565-3000
  • customized information packages
  • presence at local health fairs
  • support one-on-one, and in a group setting via local community support groups
  • opportunities to get involved in your community
  • educational workshops and conferences for individuals and families

** Note Parkinson Canada does not provide direct financial assistance, however, we may refer you to resources within the community.

Parkinson Canada communicates with you in English or French. If you require materials and support in another language, please contact info@parkinson.ca or call 1-800-565-3000.
