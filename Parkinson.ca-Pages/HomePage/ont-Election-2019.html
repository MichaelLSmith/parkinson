Accessible Voting in Ontario
Parkinson Canada, along with others representing the disability community, worked last year with Elections Ontario to ensure that voting is as accessible as possible. Read Elections Ontario's new options for accessible voting: https://www.elections.on.ca/en/voting-in-ontario/accessible-voting.html
