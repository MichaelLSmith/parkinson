And whether you are caring with or for that person, it is guaranteed to involve you more and more over time and increase your understanding of the experience of another person.

Sail On with Parkinson's - A Care Partner Story

In 2010, Steve Van Vlaenderen realized his lifelong dream of buying a sailboat. A year later, Steve was diagnosed with Parkinson’s disease and he was devastated. Doctors told him the sailboat was not a good idea.

It was hard for his partner, Darlene Hildebrand, to watch.

“I just wanted to encourage him to keep going and not give up, because I knew what he was capable of,” Hildebrand said.

Two years later he made the decision to take back control of his life. In 2017, Steve and Darlene started Sail On with Parkinson’s, a fundraiser for Parkinson Canada. Over two summers, they sailed 3,200km on the Great Lakes starting in Duluth, Minnesota finishing in Gananoque, Ontario in the summer of 2019.
