Caregivers Need Care

This session is for caregivers only and includes guided workshops on sharing and networking (1:30-4:00pm).
  • Discuss ways to help deal with the challenges of your caregiver role
  • Manage the related stress and emotions
  • Facilitated sharing, discussion and brainstorming between all attendees
