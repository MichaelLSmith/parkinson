Parkinson Canada strives to make an impact on the Canadian Parkinson's research community by working as investors in Canadian Parkinson's research potential. By funding meaningful and innovative projects and promising young researchers in their professional development, Parkinson Canada aims to encourage continued growth and revitalization in the fields of Parkinson's research in Canada.
