PD & Dance Webinar

Presented by:
David Leventhal and Rachel Bar

Thursday January 31, 2019 13:00 EST

This webinar will discuss the benefits of dance for people with Parkinson's disease. Specifically, the discussion will focus on the physical and psycho-social benefits of dance for people with Parkinson's that have been captured through research and the key attributes of dance that make a valuable and supportive intervention for people living with Parkinson's and their families.
